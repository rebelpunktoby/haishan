PracticeStatement

My work begins where the bar line loosens its grip — where harmony blurs into color and a room becomes part of the instrument. I move between location recording, live performance, and film scoring, drawn always to the same question: how sound shapes the way we feel and perceive.

Trained across conservatory composition, jazz, and music technology. The result is music rooted in impressionism but restless toward the modern — flowing, unresolved, and a little resistant to category.
