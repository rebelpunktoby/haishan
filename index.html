PracticeStatement

My creative begins where the bar line loosens its grip — where harmony blends into color and a room becomes part of the instrument. Always discovering how sound shapes the way we feel and perceive the world.

Trained across conservatory composition, jazz, and music technology. The result is music rooted in tradition but restless toward the future.
